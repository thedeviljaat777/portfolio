About Me

Passionate about teaching & shaping tomorrow's technologists.

I am an enthusiastic and dedicated Assistant Professor in the Department of Computer Science with a strong foundation in both theoretical principles and practical application. My academic journey — from BCA to MCA — has instilled in me a deep appreciation for structured learning, problem-solving, and continuous growth.

Having cleared the UGC-NET in Computer Science and Applications, I bring a research-oriented perspective to the classroom. I believe education is most powerful when it is interactive, contextual, and student-centred — transforming passive learners into confident, critical thinkers.

My teaching spans Programming, Data Structures, DBMS, and Web Technologies, and I consistently strive to connect academic concepts with real-world applications that inspire curiosity and competence.
